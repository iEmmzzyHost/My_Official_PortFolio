Nice to meet you, I am Emmanuel Hosea. When I gained admission into University of Abuja in 2018 to study computer science I was confident in pursuing my career as a software developer. At that point in my life, I became a full member of tech groups and I welcomed anything that has to do with tech. I have fall in love with WebDesign in ways I never imagined I could. I yearned to share this beauty with those around me.

I joined a volunteer organization called GADS , where I spent weekends coding and hacking in the organized meetups. I knew after my first session that becoming a WebDeveloper was also what I was meant to do with my life.

I'm an overly sensitive introvert. I have the ability to pick up on subtle, minute details that others miss, and strive to discover designs in everything. I find true happiness when I see others joy, and I want to be a part of the most important joys in your life. I would be delighted to get to know you, and project your very own genuine websites.
